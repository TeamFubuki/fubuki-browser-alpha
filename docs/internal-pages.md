# Internal Pages

Internal pages are served by `native/src/cef/FubukiSchemeHandler.cc`.

Current pages:

- `fubuki://newtab/`
- `fubuki://history/`
- `fubuki://bookmarks/`
- `fubuki://downloads/`
- `fubuki://settings/`
- `fubuki://debug/`

The pages are intentionally compact HTML generated by native code. They read SQLite-backed data
directly for simple lists and use `fubuki://settings/set?...` actions for trusted operations such
as removing history entries, clearing downloads, opening DevTools, and updating settings.

Keep these pages simple: searchable lists, useful empty states, and direct actions. Avoid complex
popover UI or a full settings clone until the backing native behavior exists.
